Events For Students

Join Us at Open Day!

University is your new beginning and choosing the right one for you can be hard. That’s why we’d love to welcome you to one of our Open Days where you can see for yourself what we have to offer. You can speak with our supportive academics, chat with current students, learn more about our courses and block teaching, explore our award-winning campus, see our guaranteed accommodation options that are all within walking distance, and ranked 11th best in the UK by Uni Compare, and visit the multicultural and vibrant student city of Leicester.
